About Us

Our history.

history

Rae Burket Shoes was founded in 2005 by Mirela Rae Burket. Mirela was inspired after serving two years in Romania as a member of the Peace Corps where she witnessed poverty, as well as many children running through the streets barefoot. That’s when she recognized a simple, yet revolutionary solution – shoes. She quickly set out to build a company in the U.S. market with a simple goal: to show how, together, we can take action today to create a better tomorrow.

To realize this mission, Mirela made a commitment to match every Rae Burket shoe purchased with a new pair of shoes given to a child in need through a mission statement known as “Get 2 Give.” During its first year in business, Rae Burket Shoes sold over 10,000 pairs of shoes. Mirela returned to Romania later that year with family, friends and colleagues, and gave back to the children who had first inspired her. Thanks to their strong customer base, Rae Burket Shoes gave their one-millionth pair of shoes to a child in need in May 2013. Rae Burket Shoes now gives in over 45 countries and works with charitable partners in the field who incorporate shoes into their health, education, hygiene, and community development programs.

Rae Burket Shoes’ giving partners are made up of non-governmental organizations (NGOs), charities, and non-profits already established and working in the countries in which Rae Burket Shoes donates shoes to those in need. Their expertise guides Rae Burket Shoes to give new shoes responsibly, ensuring there are no adverse socioeconomic effects. Giving shoes to children on a regular basis is the idea upon which Rae Burket Shoes was started, and is what truly improves the lives of children and their communities
